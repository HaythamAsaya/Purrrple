How to play
  • Purrrple is your virtual pet. You will be interacting with Purrrple through the console. Your keyboard is your way to get things done.
  • Purrrple lives in your browser and saves progress automatically. Your mission is to level up and help Purrrple grow and evolve!
  • Your level indicator is on the top left corner of the screen underneath your username.
  • Your energy indicator is underneath your level indicator. You generate power and points by populating the console.
Last but not least, you can customize the layout with your favorite colors. Don't neglect Purrrple. Good Luck!
